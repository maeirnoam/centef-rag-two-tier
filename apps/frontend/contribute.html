Upload Guidelines

  • All content should be relevant to counter-terrorism financing research
  • Documents: PDF, DOCX, DOC, PNG, JPG, JPEG, SRT (max 50MB per file)
  • Videos: MP4, M4A, MOV, AVI, MKV (max 100MB per file)
  • Audio: WAV, MP3, M4A, FLAC, OGG (max 50MB per file)
  • YouTube: Valid YouTube URLs (youtube.com/watch?v= or youtu.be/)
  • Video and audio files will be automatically transcribed and translated
  • Processing time varies based on file size, complexity, and audio length
  • You will receive notifications when processing is complete
